Japanese | English

After completing an exchange program at Meiji University in Tokyo as a New Colombo Plan Scholarship recipient, I was able to achieve fluency in Japanese, passing the Japanese Language Proficiency Test N2, a benchmark for high-level fluency and industry level comprehension. I hope to use these skills and connections in the Tokyo fashion scene to collaborate with local brands on cutting-edge digital experiences.

Futurism | Nostalgia

Through all of my work, whether it be music or creative direction, I always weave in themes of retro-futurism. My love for web design is also rooted in this duality. I aim to take the ornate design and ethereal energy of early-2000s Flash websites and translate them into a modern framework. Im currently working with Sydney-based fashion label From The 13th to create a digital look-book experience for their new collection that will aim to incorporate this duality.
